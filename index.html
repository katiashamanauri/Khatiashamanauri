About Me

Hello, I'm Khatia — a freelance journalist and storyteller

I tell stories from Eastern Europe and beyond, using data, OSINT, and AI tools to investigate, explain, and report. As a journalist with experience across TV, radio, and online platforms, I feel at ease in diverse media formats. My coverage spans politics, the economy, human rights, and healthcare - both in Georgia and beyond.
